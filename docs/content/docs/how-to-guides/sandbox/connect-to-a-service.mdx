---
title: "Connect to a service"
description: "Reach a service running inside a sandbox — a web app, API, or database — from your local machine or another process."
---

Connect to a service when a process inside the sandbox listens on a port and you need to reach it from outside.

## Expose the port

Declare the port on the image so the sandbox exposes it when it boots.

## Open a tunnel

Forward the exposed port out of the sandbox so it is reachable from your machine or another process.

## Connect to the service

Point your client at the forwarded address and use the service as if it were running locally.
